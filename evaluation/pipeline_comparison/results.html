The Key Idea

CLDN18.2 is a membrane protein. The current pipeline detects all nuclei and measures DAB across the entire cell — diluting the membrane signal with cytoplasm and nucleus.

Our model was trained using DAB membrane staining as self-supervised ground truth. It learns to detect only cells that express CLDN18.2 on their membrane — effectively acting as a combined cell segmenter and epithelial selector. It then measures DAB specifically in the membrane ring where the protein actually is.

Why this matters The baseline counts all cells (including stroma, lymphocytes, non-expressing epithelium) and averages DAB across the whole cell. This systematically under-counts positivity and under-scores intensity — potentially denying patients access to zolbetuximab.

Why the Numbers Are So Different

Baseline PipelineOur Model
What it detects All nuclei (stroma, lymphocytes, epithelial, everything) Only cells with DAB membrane staining (CLDN18.2-expressing epithelial cells)
Where it measures DAB Entire cell (nucleus + cytoplasm + membrane averaged) Membrane ring only (10px zone at cell boundary, bar-filter weighted)
Cell count ~236,000–328,000 per slide ~42,000–73,000 per slide (epithelial only)
Denominator for scoring All cells including non-tumor Only CLDN18.2-expressing cells
Effect on positivity % Diluted by non-expressing cells in denominator Higher — denominator is already filtered to expressing cells
Effect on intensity score Diluted by non-membrane DAB averaging Concentrated — measures exactly where the protein is
The model is an implicit epithelial cell selector Trained on DAB membrane staining as ground truth (min 30% membrane coverage), the model learned to detect only cells that express CLDN18.2 on their membrane. This is biologically correct: the clinical scoring denominator should be tumor cells, not all cells. The baseline pipeline includes hundreds of thousands of stromal cells, lymphocytes, and non-expressing epithelium that dilute the score.

Clinical Implications

Zolbetuximab eligibility requires ≥75% of tumor cells with ≥2+ membranous staining.

What Needs Validation

  1. Pathologist concordance — 30 tiles scored by Dr. Fiset (0/1+/2+/3+ per cell). This is the ground truth that validates everything else. Non-negotiable before clinical use.
  2. Threshold recalibration — The 0.10/0.20/0.35 thresholds were designed for whole-cell DAB. Membrane-ring DAB operates on a different scale. New thresholds must be derived from pathologist reference data.
  3. Full cohort run — These results are from 2 control slides. Need to run on all 251 slides to confirm the pattern holds across staining variation, tissue types, and borderline cases.
  4. Negative control check — Run on known CLDN18.2-negative tissue. The model should detect very few cells (confirming the epithelial selection hypothesis).
